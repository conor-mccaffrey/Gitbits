How we ended up in Ghent

The INTRA process

ip

The INTRA process gives you two types of options really:

  1. Make your first steps into Industry
  2. Stay within Research
You never know at the time, but the choice you make here can actually have a huge bearing on what you do after you get your degree. Companies will more likely hire people who already have industry experience over people that done a Research-based project. Still, the experience in terms of 'life-benefit' for doing a placement abroad far outweights the ~5 months GMP experience somebody has over you after you get your degree.
