How to Order Food/Drinks/Coffee and not look like an Idiot


Probably one of the most important things to be able to do in Berlin is to navigate certain situations without making it look obvious you haven't a clue what is going on. This involves being able to walk into a restaurant/cafe and order meals in German. Even if you aren't great at speaking German, they will appreciate the effort (I have first-hand experience in this, trust me!).
Below is a short table on the absolute basics of ordering:
